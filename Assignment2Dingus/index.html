What is a DINGUS?

A dingus, as the Urban Dictionary so eloquently states, is "Someone who never knows what's going on but you love them anyway"

Dinguses(Dingi?) come in many forms, but are most commonly:

  • Men who can't spell broccoli
  • Men who choke on pretzels
  • Orange men with tiny hands
